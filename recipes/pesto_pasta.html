Pesto Pasta

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make pesto pasta. It is a delicious Italian dish.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces spaghetti, reserve 1/2 cup starchy pasta water
  • 1/3 cup to 1/2 cup basil pesto
  • Extra virgin olive oil to drizzle
  • Fresh lemon juice, as desired
  • 4 cups arugula
  • 2 tablespoons pine nuts
  • Pinches of red pepper flakes
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Freshly grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water. Reserve 1/2 cup of the starchy water before draining. Drain the pasta and toss with olive oil.
  2. In a large skillet over very low heat, add the pesto, 1/4 cup of the reserved pasta water and stir to combine. Add the pasta and toss to coat, and more pasta water as needed to create a loose sauce.
  3. Turn the heat off. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper, to taste. Then, add the arugula and toss to barely wilt. Garnish with the red pepper flakes and pine nuts and serve.
